Prajwal Gatti

I am a Research Assistant at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur advised by Dr. Anand Mishra. My research interests lie in the area of multimodal artificial intelligence (AI) algorithms. Specifically, I am interested in cross-modal retrieval and multimodal data-to-text generation problems.

Previously, I received my B.E. in Information Science and Engineering at Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering. I also briefly interned at the Center for Neuroscience, Indian Institute of Science where I was advised by Dr. Sridharan Devarajan.
